Description

These dessert waffles are crispy on the outside, fluffy on the inside, and perfect for topping with your favorite sweet treats. Great for breakfast or dessert!

Ingredients

  • 250g all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 0.5g tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 420ml milk
  • 120ml melted butter or vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Your choice of toppings (e.g; whipped cream, fresh berries, chocolate syrup, powdered sugar, or ice cream.)

Steps

  1. Preheat your waffle maker according to its instructions
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs, then add milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract and mix well
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined (a few lumps are okay)
  5. Lightly grease the waffle iron if needed. Pour about Â½ cup of batter onto the iron and cook until golden and crispy (about 3-5 minutes, depending on your waffle maker)
  6. Continue cooking until all the batter is used
  7. Serve the waffles warm with your favorite toppings like whipped cream, fresh berries, or chocolate syrup
  8. Dust with powdered sugar for extra sweetness
  9. Dessert waffles are best enjoyed fresh and warm
  10. Pair with a scoop of ice cream for an indulgent treat
