Description

A smash burger is a juicy, flavorful burger made by smashing a ball of ground beef onto a hot griddle or skillet. This technique creates a crispy, caramelised crust while keeping the inside tender. Perfect for a quick, delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • 225g ground beef (80/20 fat ratio)
  • 2 burger buns
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional toppings: cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayo

Steps

  1. Prep Ingredients
  2. Split the ground beef into 2 equal balls
  3. Preheat a cast-iron skillet or griddle over medium high heat until very hot
  4. Lightly toast the buns on the skillet or in a toaster and set aside
  5. Place one beef ball on the skillet, immediately press it down firmly with a spatula or a flat object (like a skillet pres) to form a thin patty
  6. Sprinkle the patty generously with salt and pepper
  7. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the edges are crispy and browned, and flip the patty
  8. If using cheese, place a slice on the patty and let it melt for 30 seconds
  9. Repeat steps 5-8 for the second patty
  10. Place the patties on the toasted buns, add your favourite topping
