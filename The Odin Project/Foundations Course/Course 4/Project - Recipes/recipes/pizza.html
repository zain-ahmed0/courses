Description

This recipe will guide you through making a classic homemade pizza with a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, and your favorite toppings. It’s simple, delicious, and perfect for a fun cooking project!

Ingredients

  • 300g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 7g active dry yeast
  • 180ml warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 240g tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 150g shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (e.g; pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, etc.)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth and cover and let it rise for 1 hour
  2. Preheat your oven to 245°C. If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up
  3. Mix tomato sauce, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Set aside
  4. On a floured surface, roll the dough into a 12-inch circle or your desired shape
  5. Place the dough on a parchment-lined baking sheet or pizza peel. Spread the sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust
  6. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add your favorite toppings
  7. Transfer the pizza to the oven (or onto the preheated pizza stone). Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for 2-3 minutes and slice and serve hot
  9. Drizzle with olive oil or sprinkle fresh basil for extra flavor
